MCRC Afghan Refugee Launch Program

Sponsored by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation

About Us

This program is a comprehensive three-month initiative specially designed to support and empower newly arrived Afghan refugees as they embark on their journey of resettlement in the United States. Through this program, we take the time to get to know the refugees, understanding their unique needs, aspirations, and any challenges they may be facing and carve out a path of succession. Refugees receive financial assistance to meet immediate necessities like housing, food, and essential utilities, thereby relieving some of the initial burdens they may face upon arrival. The primary aim is to enable them to secure employment opportunities that align with their skills and aspirations, leading to economic independence and self-reliance.
